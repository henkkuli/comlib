fn inverse_power(self) -> usize

The power a value needs to be raised to in order to get its modular inverse.

If the modulus is a prime, then this is modulus() - 2 by Fermat's little theorem. For composite numbers this must be φ(modulus()) - 1 where φ is the Euler's totient function which gives the number of integers smaller than modulus() which are coprime with modulus().
